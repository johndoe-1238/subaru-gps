How to Update Subaru GPS Map

Update Now Get Support

Subaru GPS systems are known for their reliability, ease of use, and advanced navigation capabilities. But to make the most of your Subaru GPS, keeping the maps updated is crucial. Roads change, new highways are built, traffic patterns evolve—and without the latest maps, you might end up on the wrong route. This guide will walk you through the step-by-step process of updating your Subaru GPS maps, ensuring you never miss a turn.

Why Map Updates Matter

Map updates are more than just visual changes to your navigation system. They offer several key benefits that can significantly enhance your driving experience:

  • Accurate Navigation: Updated maps reflect the latest roads, addresses, and points of interest.
  • Better Routing: Updates improve the accuracy of ETA and suggest faster, smarter routes.
  • Enhanced Safety: Real-time alerts for new traffic signs, speed limits, and detours.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Less time lost on detours means better fuel economy.

Pre-Update Checklist

Before starting the update process, make sure you have the following:

  • A USB drive with at least 8GB of free space (16GB recommended)
  • A stable internet connection
  • Your vehicle with the Subaru GPS system installed
  • Access to a computer (Windows or Mac)

Step-by-Step Guide to Update Subaru GPS

Step 1: Insert USB into the GPS System

Start your vehicle and turn on your Subaru infotainment system. Insert a blank USB drive into the USB port. Go to the settings menu and select the option for system or map updates. The system will copy the identification files onto your USB.

Step 2: Connect USB to Computer

Remove the USB drive from the car and insert it into your computer. This will allow the Subaru map update software to recognize your device model and current map version.

Step 3: Download the Update Software

Visit the official Subaru navigation portal or trusted update service and download the map update tool. The software might be named “Garmin Express” or “Naviextras Toolbox,” depending on your device model.

Step 4: Check for Available Updates

Launch the map update tool and wait for it to detect your USB drive. The software will scan your system and display any available updates, including map upgrades, new POIs, or firmware improvements.

Step 5: Purchase or Install Free Updates

Some Subaru GPS systems come with lifetime free updates, while others may require a one-time purchase or subscription. If you qualify for free updates, proceed directly to installation. Otherwise, follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

Step 6: Download and Transfer Maps

Click on "Update" to download the latest maps. The process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to over an hour depending on your internet speed and the size of the update. Once downloaded, the software will transfer the files to your USB drive.

Step 7: Install Maps in the Vehicle

Safely eject the USB from your computer and reinsert it into the Subaru GPS unit. The system will automatically detect the updated files and prompt you to install them. Confirm the update and let the installation complete. Do not turn off the vehicle or remove the USB until the process is finished.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you run into problems during the update process, here are a few tips:

  • USB not recognized: Format the USB drive to FAT32 and try again.
  • Update interrupted: Restart the process from the beginning and ensure the vehicle is on during installation.
  • No update found: Make sure your Subaru model is supported and the correct software is used.

When Should You Update?

It’s recommended to update your Subaru GPS maps at least once or twice a year. However, if you frequently drive in newly developed areas or rely on the GPS for commercial use, more frequent updates are beneficial.

Benefits of Keeping Maps Current

Regular updates ensure you're not just navigating safely, but also efficiently. You avoid construction zones, road closures, and outdated routes. Moreover, updated maps often include new features or UI improvements, enhancing the overall experience.

Need Help?

If you find the process too complex or run into unexpected issues, support is available. Whether it’s guidance for downloading, installation errors, or map compatibility, professional assistance can make the process hassle-free.
